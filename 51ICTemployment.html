CHAPTER 5: The effect of using ICT

5.1: ICT and Employment

The improvement of technology and ICT really impact the employment of workers. This is because technology has lots of advantages and abilities that human cannot have.

  1. IT led to unemployment in many areas
    • Manufacturing - robots have taken over many tasks and are much faster
    • Computer controlled warehouses â€“ automatic, does not need people
    • Labour intensive work - printing industry and filing
  2. New jobs have been created in ICT
    • Writing software
    • Maintenance of robots
    • Internet related jobs
  3. Effects on People:
    • People need to be retrained due to new technology
    • Cleaner working environment
    • De-skilling of workforce
    • Computers can now do tasks that required high skill level
    • Safer working environment
    • Fewer manual tasks such as heavy lifting
  4. Effect on Companies:
    • Can employ less people
    • Robots do not take holiday, get sick or need breaks
    • Greater consistency in products made
